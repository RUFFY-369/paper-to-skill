SageAttention-2: Efficient Attention with Thorough Outlier Smoothing and Per-thread INT4 Quantization

Input Tensors:
- Q: [B, S, H, D] with float16
- K: [B, S, H, D] with float16
- V: [B, S, H, D] with float16

Output Tensors:
- O: [B, S, H, D] with float16

Hardware Requirements:
- Ampere SM86 or newer
- 24GB VRAM
- Framework: PyTorch 2.4+ with Triton 3.0, CUDA 12.x

Objective:
Compute O = softmax(smooth(Q) * smooth(K)^T / sqrt(d)) * V using INT8 QK and FP8 PV quantization.
