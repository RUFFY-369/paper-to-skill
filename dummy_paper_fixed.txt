# RESEARCH PAPER EXTRACT: Novel Latent Diffusion Backbone (FIXED EXTRACT)

## Abstract
We propose a novel, highly optimized continuous-time latent diffusion backbone for accelerated image generation.

## Tensor Definitions
- `input_latent`: The incoming latent tensor. Shape: [B, 4, 64, 64], Dtype: float32.
- `timestep`: The noise scheduling step. Shape: [B], Dtype: int64.
- `output_latent`: The denoised latent tensor. Shape: [B, 4, 64, 64], Dtype: float32.

## Hardware Constraints
- Minimal VRAM requirement: 12GB.
- CUDA Architecture Target: Ampere or newer.
- Acceleration Framework: Requires TensorRT 10.x.

## Objective Function
- Loss function: Mean Squared Error (MSE) computed between predicted noise and true noise.
